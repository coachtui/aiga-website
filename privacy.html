Privacy Policy

AIGA LLC  ·  Effective April 2026

Overview

AIGA LLC ("AIGA," "we," "us") operates aigaai.com. This policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. We keep this simple because it is simple — we're a small company, not a data broker.

What We Collect

We only collect information you actively provide through our contact form:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Company or organization (optional)
  • Area of interest
  • Message content

We do not use tracking pixels, third-party analytics, or advertising cookies. We do not collect any information passively beyond what your browser sends to any web server (standard access logs, which we do not retain or analyze).

How We Use It

We use the information you submit solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not add you to marketing lists, sell your information, or share it with any third party for their own purposes.

Form Processing

Our contact form is processed by Formspree, a third-party form service. When you submit the form, your data is transmitted to and temporarily stored by Formspree before being delivered to us. Formspree acts as a data processor on our behalf and is subject to their own Privacy Policy. We do not grant Formspree permission to use your data for any purpose beyond delivering your message to us.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions only as long as needed to respond and follow up. We do not maintain a permanent database of contact inquiries. If you'd like your information removed, email us and we'll delete it.

Your Rights

You can request to access, correct, or delete any personal information you've submitted to us at any time. Just send an email to info@aigaai.com with your request and we'll handle it promptly.
